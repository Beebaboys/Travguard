WOMEN'S SAFETY TIPS

Choose Safe Accommodation

Ensure your accommodation is in a safe neighborhood and has good reviews from other travelers. Use reputable booking websites to make reservations.

Stay Aware of Your Surroundings

When exploring a new place, be aware of your surroundings. Avoid poorly lit or secluded areas, and trust your instincts.

Keep Valuables Secure

Carry only essential valuables, use anti-theft bags, and consider wearing a money belt. Use hotel safes for passports and important documents.

Learn Local Customs

Research and respect local customs, dress codes, and social norms. Learning basic local phrases can also be helpful.

Transportation safety

Use licensed and reputable transportation services. Avoid traveling alone late at night, especially in unfamiliar areas. Keep your personal belongings secure while on public transportation.

Use technology to your advantage

Use maps and navigation apps to stay on track. Stay in touch with friends or family members by sharing your location or setting up regular check-ins.

Choose accommodations wisely

Stay in well-reviewed, reputable hotels or hostels in safe neighborhoods. Consider accommodations that offer 24/7 security.
